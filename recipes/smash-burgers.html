Smash Burgers

Dish Description

Smash burgers are the best way to make classic cheeseburgers.

You can create a homemade burger sauce to further enhance the flavor.

Ingredients Needed

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Roll ten grams of beef into a ball shape
  2. Use a flat object to smash down into a patty
  3. Sprinkle patty with salt and pepper over high heat
  4. Flip and add cheese after three minutes
  5. Let sit for 2 minutes before serving

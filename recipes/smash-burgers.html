Chicken Alfredo

Dish Description

A creamy and classic dish, chicken alfredo is a timeless family favorite sure to satsify everyone's palete.

Ingredients Needed

  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat minced garlic and heavy cream
  2. Boil pasta of choice
  3. Add ingredients together at medium to high heat
  4. Cook for 8 minutes, stirring occassionally
  5. Let sit for 2 minutes before serving
